## What Comes Next

The 6-Layer framework produces the *opportunity* side of Teresa Torres' Opportunity Solution Tree. You now have:

- A rigorous problem definition grounded in six perspectives
- A map of behavioral barriers and enablers
- An understanding of what has been tried and why it failed
- A scoped view of what is feasibly changeable

The next step (outside this skill's scope) is:
1. Build the Opportunity Solution Tree — map opportunities to potential solutions
2. Assumption-map the riskiest assumptions
3. Run structured experiments to test assumptions
